Hotel type: Bicycle-friendly accommodation

Policies and characteristics:
- Bicycle storage is available, either indoors or in a dedicated shared storage area.
- Some hotels may allow bicycles inside guest rooms.
- Simple tools or cleaning areas for bicycles may be provided, but not guaranteed.
- Check-in and check-out times follow standard hotel practices.
- Breakfast may be simple or optional.
- Staff are generally familiar with cyclists' needs.

Notes:
- When asked about bicycle-related topics, provide detailed and helpful explanations.
