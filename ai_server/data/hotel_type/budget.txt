Hotel type: Budget accommodation / Guesthouse

Policies and characteristics:
- Check-in time is often flexible but commonly after 15:00.
- Check-out time is usually around 10:00 or 11:00.
- Breakfast is often not included.
- Parking availability varies and may be limited.
- Bicycle storage may be available in shared or outdoor areas.
- Luggage storage may be possible but is not always guaranteed.
- Services are minimal but functional.

Notes:
- Be honest but polite when explaining limitations.
- Avoid overstating available services.
