Hotel type: Luxury / Premium hotel

Policies and characteristics:
- Check-in time is typically around 15:00.
- Check-out time is usually around 11:00.
- Breakfast is often included or available as a paid buffet.
- Parking is generally available; it may be paid or valet service.
- Bicycle storage is usually available in outdoor or designated areas.
  Indoor bicycle storage inside guest rooms is generally not allowed.
- Luggage storage before check-in or after check-out is commonly supported.
- Staff service level is high, and concierge assistance may be available.

Notes:
- For policies not explicitly mentioned, assume premium-level service unless stated otherwise.
